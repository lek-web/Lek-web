5 STEPS TO PREVENT FOR CORONA

1. Make a plan

Creat a plan for school,work and home:

  • Make a list of people and organisation who can help if u become sick.
  • Join a neighbourhood website or social media page to stay connected.
    • Plan ways to care for family members at risk for serious complications.

2.Prepare as you would for a winter storm

There is no need to buy large quantities of supplies:

  • Pick up some extra foods like canned goods,dry pista and peanut butter.
  • Have soap,hand sanitizer,tissues,fever reducers like acetaminophen on hand.

3.Get ready for possible changes in daily shedules

At school:

  • Make plans to care for your children if schools are closed temporarily.
  • Make plans for alternate after-school care in case they are closed temporarily.

At work:

  • Ask to work from home or take leave if you or someone in your household gets sick.

4.Stay informed

  • Stay connected on your state and local health department's social media pages.
  • Be aware of the information circulating on internet.
  • If you decide to attend a public event,practice good health habits.

5.Prevent the spread of colds,flu and COVID-19

Everyone should:

  • Stay home when you are sick.
  • Wash your hands with soap and water for atleast 20 seconds.
  • Cover your cough and sneezes with a tissue and discard in a closed container.
  • Clean frequently touched surfaces and objects.
  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
